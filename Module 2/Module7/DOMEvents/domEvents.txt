Common HTML DOM events:
Mouse Events:
    Mousedown
    Mouseup
    click
    dblclick
    mouseover
    mouseout
    mousemove

Keyboard Events:
    keydown
    keypress
    keyup

Window Events:
    load
    unload
    beforeunload
    abort
    error
    resize
    scroll
    contextmenu

Form Events:
    focus
    blur
    change
    select
    reset
    submit

Modern browsers provide drag events like dragstart, dragend, drop, etc. and touch devices provide touchstart, touchmove, and touchend.
